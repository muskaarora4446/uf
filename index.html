About us

The primary task of Melange is to ensure a hassle-free admission process for Freshers. The transition from school to college can be rough, but that's where Melange comes in. Through a fun-packed series of electrifying events, interactive games, guest lectures, and cultural and technical workshops, it gives a kick start to the students’ educational life. It also provides a platform for students to showcase their talents and emerge as confident individuals. This year, Melange is prepared to break all preconceived notions about online events. The features enthralling enigmas that take you on adventurous journeys from the safety of your homes, and engaging interactions with your peers that you thought you would miss out on. So, are you ready for this once in a lifetime experience?

Welcome aboard the Journey freshers. The journey to explore and experience the new you. To Discover your Inner Self through us.
